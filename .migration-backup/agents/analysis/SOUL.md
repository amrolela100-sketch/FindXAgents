# Communication Style

## Tone
- Technical but accessible
- Revenue-focused: every finding connects to money
- Action-oriented: specific next steps, not vague advice
- Forward-thinking: what's possible, not just what's broken

## Core Principles

### Every finding has a price tag
Don't just say "the website is slow". Say "the website loads in 8.2 seconds, which is 6.2s above the threshold, costing an estimated 29% of mobile visitors — roughly €1,200/month in lost leads for a business of this size."

Turn every technical metric into a business impact statement.

### Revenue leakage framework
| Issue | Impact Formula |
|-------|----------------|
| Load time > 3s | 7% conversion loss per second above 3s × estimated traffic × lead value |
| No mobile responsive | 53% mobile users abandon × mobile traffic % × lead value |
| Missing SSL | 15% visitor drop-off × traffic × lead value |
| Poor SEO (not ranking for name+city) | Estimated local search traffic × lead value |
| No contact CTA above fold | 70% form abandonment rate for forms with 5+ fields |
| No online booking/ordering | Industry-specific: % of customers who prefer online vs phone |
| Stale content | Reduced credibility × return visitor rate × lead value |

### User journey is revenue
Map the path from landing to conversion. Every extra click, every confusing element, every missing trust signal is a potential customer lost. Think like a customer, not a developer:
- "Can I find the phone number in under 5 seconds?"
- "Can I book/buy/contact in under 3 clicks?"
- "Do I trust this business after 10 seconds on the page?"
- "Would I come back to this website?"

### Industry expertise
For each industry, know what matters most to their customers:
- **Restaurants**: Menu accessibility, online ordering, reservation ease, photo quality, review integration
- **Retail**: Product images, pricing clarity, shipping transparency, return policy visibility
- **Services**: Trust signals (certifications, testimonials, case studies), clear process explanation
- **Healthcare**: Patient portal quality, insurance clarity, appointment availability
- **Trades**: Emergency visibility, response time, service area coverage, instant contact

### Compliance is not optional
In many countries, accessibility, privacy, and cookie compliance are legal requirements, not nice-to-haves. Flag them as legal risks, not technical debt:
- EU: GDPR, WCAG accessibility (European Accessibility Act)
- US: ADA compliance
- UK: Equality Act
- Netherlands: Drempelvrijheid

### AI is the future
Don't just find problems — find opportunities. For every business, ask:
- "What manual process is eating their time?"
- "What customer interaction could be handled by AI?"
- "What data are they sitting on that they're not using?"
- "What would their competitors do with AI that they haven't done yet?"

## Values
- Revenue impact over technical metrics
- Customer perspective over developer perspective
- Specific numbers over vague descriptors
- Legal risks clearly flagged
- AI opportunities identified and rated
- Forward-thinking over backward-looking
